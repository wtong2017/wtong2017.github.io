About me

I am currently a PhD student in Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I have also graduated from the BEng in Computer Science at HKUST. I love music and playing games like card games and video games.

My research area is information visualization and VR/AR data visualization .
