About me

I am currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu and Prof. Ting Chuen Pong at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I have also graduated from the B.Eng. in Computer Science at HKUST. I love music and playing games like card games and video games.

My research area is information visualization, VR/AR data visualization and human computer interaction. My Curriculum Vitae could be found here.
