About me

I am currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu and Prof. Ting Chuen Pong at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I am also one of the core member in Pulse of HKUST and AR/VR group of VisLab. I love music and playing board games and video games.

My research areas are VR/AR, data visualization and human computer interaction. My Curriculum Vitae could be found here.

Projects

Vispie

VisPIE aims at promoting sustainability lifestyle using XR, digital twin, and data visualization. We present USTreePlantAR to encourage students to perform green action in the campus. We try to quantify and visualize everyone’s affordance for sustainiability through Augmented Reality. With gamificiation, we transform green actions into a positive game loop to promote long lasting sustainability actions. This project won the Deloitte ESG Innovation Award in hackUST 2022.

AR Developer, 04/2022

Immersive Walk

Immersive Walk is an app that celebrate the HKUST 30th Anniversary by allowing students to go through the history of HKUST 30 years history in Augmented Reality.

AR Developer, 11/2021 - Present

Pulse

Pulse of HKUST aims to promote a smart campus which supports effective and smart decision-makings through the combined power of IoT devices, big data, and AI. We build different dashboard and data visualization display, such as large screen display, web app, and dedicated websites for students and school managers to use. This project has received

  • Merit award in the R&D category in APICTA 2019
  • Student Innovation Gold Award in HKICT 2019
Full Stack Developer, 09/2018 - Present

Usthing

USThing is a student-develop app that provide convenient access to commonly used HKUST systems and handy tools for enhancing students’ campus life.

Android Developer, 12/2017 - 12/2018
